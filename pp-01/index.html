Font 1: Merriweather

Merriweather is a typeface that I like and decided to find a pairing for. In this pairing I would use Merriweather for the body although either could be used for it. Merriweather looks less standard than Noto Serif making it a nice change for headings of certain media. It's also easier to read.

Font 2: Noto Serif

Noto was chosen as a pairing as apart of the "Seek the Similar" strategy. The two fonts are similar with some differences that can be noticed by a keen eye. Noto Serif has a somewhat standard look and can lead to lose of attention in longer bodies of text.
